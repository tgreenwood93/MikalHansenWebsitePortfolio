ABOUT ME

My name is Mikal.

Ceaseless fascination has propelled me into a career as an artist. My goal with my work is to transfer imagination into the waking world, finding a balance between emotion and aesthetic.

For years I have worked with watercolor, oils, acrylic, charcoal, and tattooing, recently finding my way into digital art as well. I love what I do, and am forever grateful for every human being I have grown with along this journey.

"Figure out who you want to be and why you are here, then do that to the best of your ability."
